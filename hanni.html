Hanni (하니) is a Vietnamese-Australian singer and member of NewJeans.

Hanni was born on October 6, 2004, in Melbourne, Australia. Her father and mother are Vietnamese. Her parents and grandparents emigrated to Australia some time before her birth. Her younger sister Jasmine was also born in Melbourne in 2007.

Hanni was a fan of One Direction as a child, and was photographed holding a sign at one of their concerts in 2013. Her bias was Niall Horan. Hanni was raised Catholic. After moving to South Korea, she was enrolled in online courses at her former high school in Australia.

In 2019, Hanni was part of an Australian K-pop dance cover group called AEMINA. In October 2019, she passed the Big Hit Global Audition and became a trainee in South Korea in 2020.
