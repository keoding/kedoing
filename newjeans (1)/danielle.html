Danielle (다니엘) is a Korean-Australian singer and member of NewJeans.

Danielle was born Danielle Marsh/Mo Ji-hye on April 11, 2005 in Newcastle, New South Wales, Australia. Her father is an Australian man named Andrew Marsh, and her mother is a South Korean native named Mo Ji-won (모지원). Danielle has one older sister, Olivia Marsh/Mo Gyu-na (모규나), who was born in 2000.

She was a student at Munsan Elementary School. When she was three years old, Danielle moved with her family to South Korea, where they remained until 2012.

In October 2019, Danielle returned to South Korea after passing BigHit's Australian Global Audition and became a trainee.
