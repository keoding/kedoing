About NewJeans!

NewJeans (Korean: 뉴진스; RR: Nyujinseu) is a five member South Korean girl-group under ADOR. They made their music video debut on July 22, 2022, and made their official debut on August 1st with their album New Jeans. They will also make their official Japanese Debut on June 25, 2024 with the single album Supernatural.
