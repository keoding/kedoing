Minji (민지) is a South Korean singer and member of NewJeans.

Minji was born Kim Min-ji on May 7, 2004 in Chuncheon, Gangwon, South Korea. During her elementary school years, she learned English before studying abroad in Canada for a few months. In 2017, Minji became a trainee under Source Music for four and a half years.

Minji's first appearance was in the videos titled "PLUS GLOBAL AUDITION #THE_GIRL 1" and "PLUS GLOBAL AUDITION #THE_GIRL 2" both uploaded on Sep 3, 2019.

Later on, Minji and Hanni both made appearances in BTS's music video "Permission to Dance" on July 8, 2021.
