Haerin (해린) is a South Korean singer and member of NewJeans.

Haerin was born Kang Hae-rin on May 15, 2006 in Seoul, South Korea. Haerin attended school until her second year of middle school in 2020, during which she dropped out to become a trainee.

She has a younger sister. She has a female Pomeranian family dog named Banggeul. Fans have pointed out that she speaks in a Gyeongsang-do dialect but Haerin clarified that she has none and she was born and raised in Seoul. In an online forum, a student claimed to have seen her take her middle school GED.[7] Haerin confirmed that she has taken GED in the 230829 NewJeans Soundwave FanSign Event.
